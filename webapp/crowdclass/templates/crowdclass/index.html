Instructions

The study involves an 30 minute long session where you will be asked to interact with a software interface of a citizen science platform. You can do the study remotely anywhere with good internet connection, but we ask that you allocate an undisturbed environment for 30 minutes. Your valuable classifications in this citizen science platform will be used by scientists to study how these galaxies formed. Please answer the questions that you are prompted with in the software without searching the web or books for reference.

Please feel free to make use of the buttons during the experiment to assist you with the task:
